Ceramic Series J601CR

J601CR offers:

Good green characteristic/ Shorter bedding-in time

A long using life

Good braking performance under high temperature and speed

A environment-friendly formula( No asbestos )

Comfortable pedal feeling and lower braking noise

Less damage on rotor and less dust after braking
